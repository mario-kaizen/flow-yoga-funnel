# Flow Yoga | Female Infrared Veo Creative

## Objective

Generate one eight second vertical video for a Flow Yoga Meta ad. The result should feel like the approved female brand concept photography brought to life, not like generic yoga stock footage.

## Visual direction

- One adult woman in matte black yoga clothing
- Deep standing forward fold with one subtle breath and a controlled settling movement
- Near black studio with coral orange infrared side light
- Tight asymmetric editorial crop with the subject on the right
- Clean negative space on the left for advertising copy added after generation
- Slow stable camera push in
- Photorealistic anatomy, skin, fabric and muscle movement
- Ambient breathing and room tone only
- No generated typography, logos or graphic overlays

## Reference inputs

Use photo only crops from the two approved female Flow concept images. Exclude the concept typography and logo from the crops. Supply both images as Veo asset references.

## Generation controls

- Model: `veo-3.1-generate-preview`
- Aspect ratio: `9:16`
- Duration: `8`
- Resolution: `1080p`
- Person generation: `allow_adult`
- Sample count: `1`

## Prompt

An eight-second vertical cinematic advertising shot for a premium infrared heated yoga and mobility studio.

A fit adult woman with long dark hair, wearing minimal matte-black yoga clothing, holds a deep wide-legged standing forward fold in a nearly black studio. Her body fills the right two-thirds of the frame. Use a tight, asymmetric crop of her upper back, shoulder, arm, waist and legs, with generous clean charcoal negative space on the left for advertising copy.

She takes one slow breath, subtly lengthens through her spine, then settles slightly deeper into the stretch. Her movement is precise, controlled and anatomically natural. A faint sheen of perspiration catches the infrared light. Her face remains partially visible in shadow and never looks toward the camera.

Match the supplied reference images closely: hushed high-fashion editorial wellness photography, deep charcoal blacks, very low-key exposure, strong contrast, sculpted coral-orange infrared side lighting across warm skin, soft shadow falloff, restrained colour palette, tactile skin detail, calm intensity, premium and slightly raw rather than glossy. The studio environment is almost completely hidden in darkness.

One continuous shot with no cuts. The camera performs an extremely slow, stable push-in. 50mm portrait lens, shallow depth of field, 24 frames per second, photorealistic skin and fabric, natural body proportions and realistic muscle movement. Finish with the woman holding the pose and negative space still visible on the left.

Ambient audio only: soft controlled breathing, a faint low room hum and subtle fabric movement. No dialogue, no music, no text, no logos and no graphic overlays.

## Negative prompt

words, letters, captions, logo, watermark, graphic overlay, bright studio, white walls, windows, daylight, mirrors, candles, incense, plants, pastel yoga aesthetic, smiling at camera, direct eye contact, dialogue, music, rapid movement, jump cuts, montage, camera shake, handheld motion, fast zoom, lens flare, neon lighting, oversaturated orange, plastic skin, sexualised framing, additional people, duplicated body parts, extra limbs, extra fingers, malformed hands, fused joints, broken anatomy, impossible pose, body warping, flicker, temporal jitter, sudden clothing changes

## Acceptance criteria

- Eight second portrait video renders successfully at 1080p
- The image reads immediately as the Flow Yoga female concept direction
- The woman moves naturally with no obvious anatomical failure
- The coral infrared lighting and near black palette remain consistent
- The left side retains usable negative space
- No visible generated words, logos or watermarks appear
- The file plays locally with both video and ambient audio streams
